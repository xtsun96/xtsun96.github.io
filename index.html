About Me

My research interests generally lie in the area of blockchain and cryptofinance. I am particularly interested in Decentralized Finance (DeFi) and Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). Currently, I focus on empirical evidence of centralization in blockchain, especially governance centralization in DAO.

Research

Voter coalitions and democracy in Decentralized Finance: Evidence from MakerDAO. Working paper (with Xi Chen, Charalampos Stasinakis and Georgios Sermpinis).
Liquidity Risks in Lending Protocols: Evidence from Aave Protocol. Working paper (with Charalampos Stasinakis and Georgios Sermpinis).
Decentralized illusion in Decentralized Finance: Evidence from tokenized voting in MakerDAO polls. Working paper (with Charalampos Stasinakis and Georgios Sermpinis).
Bribes to Miners: Evidence from Ethereum. Working paper.
