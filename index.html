About Me

My research centers on FinTech and crypto finance, specifically exploring financial risks in DeFi and investor structures within blockchain markets. Beyond academia, I am active in DeFi business practices and project incubation. I am currently seeking motivated PhD candidates interested in crypto finance, sustainable finance, or AI for finance. If you are driven to explore these frontiers together, please reach out with your CV and research proposal. I also welcome inquiries regarding potential research or industry collaborations.
